GSS — Generalised Sky Survey

A modular anomaly-detection pipeline for photometric and astrometric sky surveys. Scans SDSS, Gaia, and AllWISE on a per-tile basis, scores candidates with an Isolation Forest against the full observed population, cross-matches against SIMBAD, NED, and Gaia, and compiles evidence for review. AI-assisted evidence synthesis is layered on top of — never in place of — the deterministic scoring core.
